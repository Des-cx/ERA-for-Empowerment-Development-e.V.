About ERA e.V.

Building a fairer and more inclusive society through empowerment and cooperation.

Who We Are

ERA for Empowerment and Development e.V. is a nonprofit organization based in Dortmund. What started as a grassroots initiative was officially registered in 2025 as an association (e.V.).

Today, we work to promote equality, empowerment, and social inclusion through structured community programs.

Unsere Mission

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals, strengthen communities, and promote equal opportunities through education, cooperation, and sustainable development initiatives.

Our Core Focus

Youth & Senior Services

We bridge generations by supporting young people in developing skills and confidence while ensuring older adults remain socially included and active.

International Cooperation

We promote cultural exchange, tolerance, and understanding between communities and countries.

Development Cooperation

We support projects that improve living conditions and encourage sustainable development.

Gender Equality

We actively promote equal rights and opportunities for all individuals.

Sports Promotion

We use sports as a tool for health, teamwork, discipline, and social inclusion.

Our History
