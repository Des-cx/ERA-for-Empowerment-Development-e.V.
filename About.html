Über uns

Über ERA e.V.

Building a fairer and more inclusive society through empowerment and cooperation.

Who We Are

ERA for Empowerment and Development e.V. is a nonprofit organization based in Dortmund.

Today we promote equality, empowerment, and social inclusion through structured community programs.

Our Mission

Our Mission

We empower individuals, strengthen communities, and promote equal opportunities through education and cooperation.

Our Core Focus

Youth & Senior Support

Supporting young and older people for a dignified and active life.

International Cooperation

Promoting exchange, tolerance, and global understanding.

Development Cooperation

Improving living conditions through sustainable projects.

Gender Equality

Promoting equal opportunities for all people.

Sports Promotion

Using sports as a tool for health and social inclusion.

Our History

2025

Began as a passionate grassroots initiative focused on social change in Dortmund.

2026

Officially registered as a nonprofit association (e.V.) – A major milestone.
