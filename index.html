Who We Are

ERA for Empowerment & Development e.V. is a nonprofit organization based in Dortmund, Germany. We are dedicated to building inclusive communities where people can grow, participate, and thrive.

We focus on empowerment, equality, and sustainable social development both locally and internationally.

Learn More About Us

Our Core Areas

We work across five key areas of impact

Youth & Senior Support

We support young people in building confidence and life skills while promoting dignity, health, and social inclusion for older adults.

International Outlook

We promote tolerance, cultural exchange, and global understanding to strengthen peaceful coexistence.

Development Cooperation

We support initiatives that improve living conditions and promote sustainable community development.

Gender Equality

We work to ensure equal opportunities for everyone, regardless of gender or background.

Sports Promotion

We use sports to build teamwork, discipline, health, and social inclusion.
